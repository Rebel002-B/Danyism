Dandyism

Dandyism centers around Dandy, the gentle and whimsical creator of the Toon world. He represents joy, creativity, and unconditional love. His followers seek to spread kindness, laughter, and vibrant imagination, believing that joy is the purest form of worship. Dandy is often depicted as a dandy lion with sparkles and stripes, dancing under a rainbow sky.

Sproutism

Sproutism honors Sprout, the green guardian of life and growth. Sprout protects the innocent and shelters those in need. Worshippers of Sproutism tend gardens, nurture life, and believe in starting small to grow mighty. It's a humble and earthy path, grounded in kindness and courage.

Astroism

Astroism gazes to the stars, inspired by Astro, the dreamy visionary who walks among constellations. Astro guides dreamers and thinkers, and his followers seek wisdom through imagination, introspection, and stargazing. Astroism believes every star is a soul, and every dream a prophecy.

Shellyism

Shellyism follows Shelly, the motherly protector wrapped in blankets of compassion. She comforts the weary, shelters the lost, and teaches love in silence. Devotees of Shellyism create cozy sanctuaries and offer warmth to all who pass through their lives.

Bobetteism

Bobetteism embraces the chaos of fun and surprise. Bobette, the giggling spirit of joy and trouble, dances wildly through pranks and parties. Her followers believe in finding light in darkness through laughter, unexpected kindness, and spontaneous joy.

Veeism

Veeism is about bold choices and radiant truth. Vee blazes like the sun, pushing others to rise, fight, and shine. Her believers seek courage in challenges and speak truth even when it shakes. Her temples are fields of gold and her voice is thunder and sparkle.

Bassieism

Bassieism stands in stillness. Bassie is a calm river under a pink sky — gentle, soft, and unshakable. Her followers value peace, resilience, and deep inner strength. Meditation and soft speech are paths to understanding her mystery.

Pebbleism

Pebbleism honors Pebble, the smallest and most fierce. Though tiny, Pebble's spirit echoes like thunder. His believers find greatness in humility and power in persistence. They believe even a pebble can cause a mountain to move — with courage, laughter, and grit.

Book of Hearts

1:1 These are the laws spoken by Dandy, said with joy and not with judgment:
1:2 Love one another as flowers love the sun.
1:3 Laugh often, and your heart shall never be empty.

Book of Parables

1:1 Dandy spake unto the Toons in riddles and rhymes,
1:2 For wisdom lives in laughter and love in the dance of the fool.
1:3 Seek not the straight path, but the joyous wander.

Book of Psalms of Dandy

1:1 O Dandy, Thou who walketh in the green meadows of joy,
1:2 Hear our bubblegum praise, our songs of colors bright and light.
1:3 May our days be filled with sparkles and dreams as sweet as honey.
